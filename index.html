May 15 - May 24

Introduction

Tucker High is conducting a drive to collect old and used batteries. Many types of batteries contain hazardous materials which can lead to environmental damage if they are disposed of incorrectly. By recycling, we can deal with these substances safely and aid in preserving nonrenewable resources. If you bring in batteries, you can help to prevent heavy metals and other toxins from leeching into the ground and polluting our ecosystem.
